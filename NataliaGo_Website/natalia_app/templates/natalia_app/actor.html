Natalia Go

I started acting professionally in Philippine theatre in 2012 with a company called Gantimpala Theatre Foundation. From then, I knew that acting was something I wanted to do for the rest of my life. It may not be the only thing I do but I can't imagine myself not doing it.

My most favorite character that I played is Queen Gertrude of Hamlet. There is something uniquely exhilirating in doing a Shakespeare play. Something that can't be described by ordinary words, maybe because being in a Shakespeare play means being in a world with a language of its own.
